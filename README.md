# tetris_java
My implementation of the classis Tetris game in Java. 

---

### Controls
* Left and Right arrows for moving the block left and right
* Down arrow for moving the block down
* Q and W keys for rotating left and right

---

If you want to play again after finishing your game, you have to close and open again. This will be updated in a future patch.
